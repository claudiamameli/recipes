
# Hello World! This is a recipe for traditional hummus.

## Ingredients
- chickpeas
- salt
- black pepper
- cumin
- tahini
- lemon juice

# Instructions 
Soak the chickpeas in water for a day. Cook the chickpeas until soft. Bleand the chickpeas and season to taste.
